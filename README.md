## 🚀 Features

### POS & Sales
- ✅ Point of Sale with quick product search
- ✅ Credit Sales with customer management
- ✅ Multiple payment methods (Cash, M-Pesa, Bank Transfer, Credit)
- ✅ Real-time inventory deduction
- ✅ Transaction tokens for security

### Inventory Management
- ✅ 294 Products pre-loaded
- ✅ Stock tracking with low stock alerts
- ✅ Restock management
- ✅ Product editing (name, category, cost, price, stock)
- ✅ Bulk price updates

### Accounting
- ✅ General Ledger with double-entry bookkeeping
- ✅ Automated Journal Entries
- ✅ Account balances
- ✅ Tax Management (VAT, Withholding, Excise)
- ✅ Budget Management

### Business Management
- ✅ Suppliers Management (Payables)
- ✅ Customers Management (Receivables)
- ✅ Miscellaneous Expenses
- ✅ Audit Trail with transaction tokens
- ✅ User Management with roles

### Reports
- ✅ Sales Report (CSV, JSON, PDF)
- ✅ Credit Report
- ✅ Restock Report
- ✅ Ledger Report
- ✅ Journal Report
- ✅ Tax Report
- ✅ Summary Report

### Real-Time & Mobile
- ✅ Firebase Real-time sync
- ✅ Offline support with localStorage fallback
- ✅ PWA (Progressive Web App) - Install as mobile app
- ✅ Multi-user support
